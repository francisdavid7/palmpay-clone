    Q2. How do I become a PalmPay agent?

    Submit your information in the form at the bottom of our website (click to go), or call our hotline 018885000 . You will be contacted by a dedicated customer representative.

    Q3. What are the requirements to become a PalmPay agent?

    You will need a bank verification number (BVN) and other necessary documents to become a PalmPay agent.

    Q4. What's the fee policy if I want to get a PalmPay POS

    Different cooperation modes has different fees policies, about 0.4%-0.5% per transaction, and different transaction fees have different capping fees.

    Q6. What should I do if I have problems when using PalmPay POS?
